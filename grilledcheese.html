Grilled Cheese

To original site

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients.

Step 2 

Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of a
slice of bread.

Step 3

Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese.

Step 4 

Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of
cheese.

Step 5 

Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until
cheese is melted.

Step 6 

Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese. Serve and
enjoy!
