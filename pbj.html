Peanut Butter & Jelly

To original site

Ingredients

  • 2 slices sandwitch bread
  • 2 tablespoons peanutbutter
  • 2 teaspoons of grape jelly or 2 teaspoons of strawberry jam

Directions

Step 1

Spread the peanut butter on one piece of bread.

Step 2

Spread the jelly on the other side.

Step 3

Put the two pieces of bread together to form a sandwich.
